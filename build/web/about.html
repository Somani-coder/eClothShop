With e-commerce being one of the paramount drivers of the Indian Economy, the market has grown exponentially in the past two decades of the 21st century. The Indian Fashion Industry has been an integral part of our country's economy even before the tech industry came into existence and remains one of the major contributors to the growing economy. The collaboration between the Indian Fashion industry and the E-commerce industry has led us to see growth in both employment opportunities, annual revenue generation, and faster delivery of services and goods. Fast fashion is a very lethal practice that has caused more harm than good to mankind and the environment in the past decade. Fast fashion involves the creation of garments in large volumes with cheap materials, low labor costs, and toxic dyes for coloring which are very hazardous to human health and contaminate fresh water and land. To prevent fast fashion from being a trend, making renting and thrifting of clothes the new normal is encouraged by the youths and adults of this decade.Eco-Value Wardrobe is the online site to visit for renting or thrifting quality clothes and beautiful accessories at a reasonable price. If you're interested in putting renting and thrifting to practice, you can create your account and set up your shop to either rent or sell your goods and accessories to your customer base. The site includes a queue system for booking appointments, dashboards to check actions taken in the account, and a profile timeline that shows your contact and social media details which helps you to connect with your clients and audience, and further maintains a record of your sales with a support system to help out with navigation and other issues which you will encounter with the navigation. The visitor can make an account and have access to filter products for them based on their preferences like budget, relevance, distance, and types of fashion and accessories. They would be provided with wishlists, inbox notifications regarding discounts, new arrival, restocks, and availability, and a query system to help them with navigation and other related issues they encountered with their visits.
